# LEMMIK CONSTRUCTION RECRUITING - LOGO CONCEPTS

## CONCEPT 1: "THE BUILDER" 
### Design Elements:
- **Icon:** Stylized hard hat with gear/cog inside (representing both construction + recruitment machinery)
- **Typography:** Bold, industrial sans-serif font
- **Colors:** Deep blue (#1e3a8a) + Orange accent (#f97316)
- **Layout:** Icon left, text right
- **Tagline:** "Building Teams. Building Futures."

### Description:
Clean, professional design that immediately communicates construction industry focus. The gear inside the hard hat suggests the systematic, efficient approach of your AI-powered recruiting.

---

## CONCEPT 2: "THE CONNECTOR"
### Design Elements:
- **Icon:** Two construction cranes forming a handshake/connection
- **Typography:** Modern, clean font with slight architectural angles
- **Colors:** Steel gray (#64748b) + Bright green (#22c55e)
- **Layout:** Icon above company name, centered
- **Tagline:** "Connecting Talent with Opportunity"

### Description:
Emphasizes the relationship/networking aspect of recruiting. The crane handshake suggests both construction industry and the human connection aspect of recruitment.

---

## CONCEPT 3: "THE FOUNDATION"
### Design Elements:
- **Icon:** Stylized "L" made from construction beams/I-beams
- **Typography:** Strong, architectural font
- **Colors:** Charcoal (#374151) + Golden yellow (#fbbf24)
- **Layout:** Large "L" icon with text integrated
- **Tagline:** "Your Foundation for Success"

### Description:
Uses the "L" from Lemmik as architectural element. The I-beam construction suggests strength, foundation, and building - perfect metaphors for recruiting/career building.

---

## CONCEPT 4: "THE NETWORK" 
### Design Elements:
- **Icon:** Hexagonal pattern (like construction materials) with people silhouettes
- **Typography:** Tech-forward, clean font
- **Colors:** Navy blue (#1e40af) + Cyan (#06b6d4)
- **Layout:** Icon left, text stacked right
- **Tagline:** "Smart Recruiting. Strong Results."

### Description:
Modern, tech-savvy design emphasizing the AI/network aspect. Hexagons suggest both construction materials (bolts, structures) and digital/AI networks. Appeals to younger professionals.

---

## CONCEPT 5: "THE PROFESSIONAL"
### Design Elements:
- **Icon:** Minimalist construction triangle/pyramid with upward arrow
- **Typography:** Classic, sophisticated serif font
- **Colors:** Deep forest green (#166534) + Silver accent (#94a3b8)
- **Layout:** Icon and text in single line, balanced
- **Tagline:** "Excellence in Executive Placement"

### Description:
Most conservative/traditional design. The triangle suggests stability, growth, and construction. Clean, trustworthy appearance that appeals to established construction companies and senior executives.
