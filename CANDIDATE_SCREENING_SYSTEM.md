# Candidate Screening System

## Screening Criteria - Civil Estimator

### Tier 1: Must-Have (Auto-Reject if Missing)
- [ ] 3+ years construction estimating experience
- [ ] Heavy civil/infrastructure background  
- [ ] Experience reading construction drawings
- [ ] Legal to work in US
- [ ] Located in LA area or willing to relocate

### Tier 2: Highly Preferred (Score 1-5 points each)
- [ ] HeavyBid, HCSS, or similar software experience (5 pts)
- [ ] Caltrans/public works experience (4 pts)  
- [ ] Bachelor's degree in relevant field (3 pts)
- [ ] 5+ years experience (3 pts)
- [ ] Prevailing wage knowledge (2 pts)

### Tier 3: Nice to Have (Score 1-2 points each)
- [ ] LEED/sustainability experience (2 pts)
- [ ] Project management experience (2 pts)
- [ ] Multiple estimating software platforms (1 pt)
- [ ] Local market knowledge (1 pt)

## Scoring System
- **Tier 1:** All must be YES (or auto-reject)
- **Tier 2:** Score out of 17 points
- **Tier 3:** Score out of 6 points
- **Total Possible:** 23 points

### Interview Thresholds
- **Immediate Interview:** 18+ total points
- **Phone Screen First:** 12-17 points  
- **Follow-up Questions:** 8-11 points
- **Reject:** <8 points or missing Tier 1 requirements

## Red Flags (Auto-Reject)
- Job hopping (5+ jobs in 3 years without explanation)
- Gaps in employment >6 months without explanation
- No construction experience
- Unrealistic salary expectations (>$140k without senior experience)
- Poor communication in application materials

## Resume Screening Checklist

### Quick Scan (30 seconds)
1. Years of experience clearly stated?
2. Construction/civil background evident?
3. Estimating experience mentioned?
4. Local or willing to relocate?
5. Professional presentation?

### Deep Review (2 minutes)
1. Specific projects and dollar values
2. Software/tools mentioned
3. Education background
4. Career progression makes sense
5. Achievements/accomplishments listed

## Automated Screening Questions
1. "How many years of construction estimating experience do you have?" (Required: minimum 3)
2. "Do you have heavy civil/infrastructure estimating experience? Please describe." (Required: must show relevant experience)
3. "What estimating software are you proficient with?" (Preferred: HeavyBid, HCSS, etc.)
4. "Are you legally authorized to work in the US?" (Required: Yes)
5. "What is your desired salary range?" (Screen for realistic expectations)
6. "Are you currently located in the LA area or willing to relocate?" (Required: Yes)

## Interview Scheduling Automation

### Immediate Schedule (High Scores)
- Send calendar link with 3-5 available slots
- Include interview details and agenda
- Confirm 24 hours before

### Phone Screen First (Medium Scores)  
- 15-minute phone screening
- If pass: schedule full interview
- If fail: polite rejection with feedback

### Follow-up Process (Borderline Candidates)
- Email with specific questions about experience gaps
- Request portfolio/work samples
- Schedule based on responses
