# Free Job Posting Sites - Test Phase

## 🆓 Completely Free Options

### 1. **iHireConstruction** ⭐ TOP PICK
- **Cost:** FREE account creation and job posting
- **Why Perfect:** Construction-specific, targeted audience
- **Reach:** Construction professionals actively searching
- **Setup Time:** 10 minutes

### 2. **Facebook Jobs/Meta for Work** 
- **Cost:** FREE
- **Why Great:** Massive local reach, easy targeting by location
- **Reach:** Los Angeles construction workers
- **Setup Time:** 5 minutes (if you have Facebook business page)

### 3. **Google for Jobs** (via company website)
- **Cost:** FREE (if you have a website)
- **Why Powerful:** Shows up in Google search results
- **Setup:** Add structured data to your website
- **Reach:** Everyone searching "construction estimator jobs Los Angeles"

### 4. **ZipRecruiter** - First Job Free
- **Cost:** First job posting is FREE
- **Why Smart:** Auto-distributes to 100+ job boards
- **Reach:** Massive distribution network
- **Catch:** Only first job is free

### 5. **LinkedIn Company Page**
- **Cost:** FREE (with company page)
- **Why Valuable:** Professional network, quality candidates
- **Reach:** Construction professionals in LA
- **Setup:** Need company LinkedIn page first

## 💰 Low-Cost Options ($25-50)

### 6. **Craigslist Los Angeles**
- **Cost:** ~$25-45 depending on category
- **Why Effective:** High local visibility, construction workers check it
- **Reach:** Local Los Angeles market
- **Setup:** 5 minutes

### 7. **Recooty** - Multi-Board Distribution
- **Cost:** Free plan available, paid plans from $29/month
- **Why Amazing:** Distributes to 250+ job boards automatically
- **Reach:** Indeed, LinkedIn, Glassdoor, Monster, Google Jobs
- **Value:** One post = everywhere
