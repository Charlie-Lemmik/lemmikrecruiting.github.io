## Core Job Posting Template

**Job Title:** Civil Construction Estimator - Heavy Civil Projects
**Location:** Los Angeles, California
**Salary Range:** $75,000 - $120,000 annually (plus benefits and bonuses)
**Employment Type:** Full-time

### About This Role
We are seeking an experienced Civil Construction Estimator to join our growing team in Los Angeles. You'll be responsible for preparing accurate cost estimates for heavy civil construction projects including roads, bridges, utilities, and infrastructure developments.

### Key Responsibilities
- Prepare detailed cost estimates for heavy civil construction projects
- Review project plans, specifications, and bid documents
- Conduct quantity takeoffs and material calculations  
- Analyze subcontractor and vendor pricing
- Collaborate with project managers and field teams
- Present estimates to management and clients
- Track project costs against estimates

### Required Qualifications
- 3+ years of construction estimating experience
- Heavy civil construction background (roads, utilities, infrastructure)
- Proficiency with estimating software (preferred: HeavyBid, HCSS, or similar)
- Ability to read and interpret construction drawings and specifications
- Strong analytical and mathematical skills
- Excellent communication and presentation abilities
- Bachelor's degree in Construction Management, Civil Engineering, or equivalent experience

### Preferred Qualifications  
- California Department of Transportation (Caltrans) project experience
- Knowledge of prevailing wage requirements
- Experience with public works projects
- LEED or sustainability project experience

### What We Offer
- Competitive salary ($75,000-$120,000) based on experience
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance
- 401(k) with company matching
- Paid time off and holidays
- Professional development opportunities
- Company vehicle allowance

### Physical Requirements
Able to perform essential job functions with or without reasonable accommodation, including:
- Office work with computer use
- Occasional job site visits
- Review of construction documents
